How to Apply for UCF Housing

UCF Housing offers shared suite and apartment-style floorplans. All communities include 24-hour staff support, furnished rooms, all utilities (no caps), Wi-Fi, individual leases, and central locations. First-year students are given preference for housing on the Orlando main campus. Students of all classifications may apply for housing at Rosen College Apartments, NorthView, or an affiliated option.

STEPS
1. Choose your campus and housing agreement. (First-year students admitted for Summer B must apply for a separate summer-only agreement in addition to their Fall agreement. The Rosen agreement includes Fall and Spring with the option to add Summer.)
2. Apply for housing online: Log in to myUCF > Student Self Service > Housing > Housing Portal. Select semester, campus, and agreement type; create your profile; sign the terms and conditions; submit your prepayment to complete the application.
3. Apply for a Living Learning Community (optional) — a group of students placed together by common interest or academic program.
4. Verify your agreement status. Status changes from "submitted" to "confirmed" once space is verified; you'll receive an email and can view status in the Housing Portal.
5. Find roommates (roommate matching).
6. Online Room Selection — select your room with your roommate group from May through July; appointment times are based on when you completed your application; rooms are first-applied, first-served.
7. Move in.

KEY NOTES
- No student is guaranteed on-campus housing; apply early.
- A prepayment is required for the agreement to be accepted/confirmed.
- Confirmed students who skip online room selection are assigned by staff to remaining rooms.
- Housing agreements may be cancelled and prepayments forfeited if the admissions enrollment deposit is not paid.

GENERAL HOUSING AGREEMENT TIMELINES

Incoming Freshmen:
- Fall: Agreements open mid-November, close when full, confirm/waitlist on a rolling basis, room selection in June.
- Summer B: Open mid-November, close when full, rolling confirmation, room selection in May.
- Spring: Open early November, close late November, confirm early December, room selection mid-December.

Current Residents / Transfer / Graduate / Off-Campus Students:
- Fall Lottery (Main): Open early January, close late January, confirm/waitlist late January, room selection February/March.
- Fall Rosen: Open early January, close when full, rolling confirmation, room selection March/April.
- Summer A/B/C (Main): Open mid-February, close mid-March, confirm early April, room selection April.
- Summer A/B/C (Rosen): Open mid-February, close when full, rolling confirmation, room selection April.
- Spring: Open early November, close late November, confirm early December, room selection mid-December.
