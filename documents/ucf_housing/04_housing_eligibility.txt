Eligibility for UCF Housing

Eligibility for UCF Housing is based on your student classification and the semester(s) for which you are seeking housing. Eligibility for a particular type of housing does not guarantee availability; availability varies each year. Students may apply for UCF Housing once they are offered admission, and are encouraged to apply as early as possible. If you cannot apply for a type of housing you are eligible for, you may join a waitlist.

Married and family housing is not available at UCF. Students in the UCF Online degree program are not eligible to live in UCF Housing.

ELIGIBILITY BY CLASSIFICATION (summary)
- First-Year Students: Given preference for on-campus housing on the Orlando main campus. Academic-Year private rooms are very limited for Fall/Spring; some options are based on availability for Spring-only and Spring/Summer.
- Current Residents: May apply for Towers and NorthView (Annual, Fall/Spring/Summer) via lottery; Rosen Spring-only is "contact us."
- Off-Campus, Graduate, and Transfer Students: Most live off-campus. On-campus options are limited and primarily NorthView (lottery) and Rosen College Apartments (first-come, first-served).

CHOOSING AN AGREEMENT
All residence halls share amenities: high-speed Wi-Fi, laundry, included utilities (no caps), maintenance, and social programs.
- Academic Agreement = TWO semesters (Fall + Spring); residents must vacate during winter break. Communities: Apollo, Libra, Nike & Hercules, Neptune, Lake Claire.
- Annual Agreement = THREE semesters (Fall, Spring, following Summer); residents may stay over winter break but must commit to the full duration. Communities: Towers, NorthView, Rosen (Rosen can add a summer semester).
- Most centrally located halls: Libra and Apollo (Academic Agreement).
- Private bedrooms: Towers, NorthView, Rosen (all apartment-style).
- All-freshmen communities: Apollo, Libra, Lake Claire, Neptune, Nike & Hercules.

LIVING LEARNING COMMUNITIES (LLCs)
For first-year students to connect to a smaller community. Current LLCs: EXCEL/COMPASS, Nursing @ Nike, Honors. All LLCs except Honors are in Academic Agreement communities. Honors LLC is in Tower III and requires a Towers Agreement; Honors students are NOT given priority for on-campus housing.

TRANSFER / GRADUATE / UPPERCLASSMEN
On-campus housing is very limited. UCF recommends off-campus options. Resources: the Office of Student Neighborhood Relations (https://nrse.sswb.ucf.edu/), the Knight's Guide to Living Off Campus, and the UCF Off-Campus Partners website (https://ucf.offcampuspartners.com/). Towers and NorthView use a lottery for returning residents, upperclassmen, and off-campus students; Rosen is first-come, first-served. Apply and submit prepayment during the lottery/application window.

RATES: Rental rates are listed by semester, per person, per room type, approved by the UCF Board of Trustees each year and subject to change (https://www.housing.ucf.edu/rates/).

SUMMER B: First-year students admitted for Summer B must apply for a separate summer-only agreement in addition to their Fall agreement.
