What are my dining options?

UCF Dining Memberships (meal plans) are convenient and flexible. Anyone with a valid UCF ID may purchase a Dining Membership. All members gain access to dining locations across campus. All Dining Memberships are voluntary — there is no mandatory meal plan requirement — providing an open-access community atmosphere to study, socialize, and eat. Members get value whether they eat on campus three times a week or three times a day.

If you don't want a membership, there is a variety of dining establishments to choose from, including Chick-fil-A, Knightro's, Einstein Bros. Bagels, Huey Magoo's, Gringos Locos, Starbucks, and many others. Dining Services website: https://ucf.campusdish.com/ (also https://ucf.mydininghub.com/en)

HOW MEAL PLANS WORK
A Meal Plan includes Meal Swipes and Dining Dollars, loaded onto your UCF student ID (physical, virtual, or digital meal pass).
- Meal Swipes give access to the all-you-care-to-eat dining halls: '63 South and Knightro's.
- Dining Dollars are prepaid funds usable at any UCF Dining location across campus.
- A Meal Plan unlocks dining at locations across campus (about 25 locations cited by UCF Dining).
- Guest passes can be purchased when entering the dining halls ('63 South and Knightro's) with Dining Dollars, credit card, or cash.

MEMBERSHIP TYPES (per UCF Dining Meal Plan Terms & Conditions)
- ANY 10: Any active UCF student. Ten meals per week at '63 South and Knightro's (8-month and 4-month memberships). Meals reset every Monday; unused swipes do not carry over.
- ANY 14: Any active UCF student. Fourteen meals per week at '63 South and Knightro's. Meals reset every Monday; unused swipes do not carry over.
- "Create Your Own" memberships (for Lake Claire, Towers, or off-campus residents; resident status verified):
   - KNIGHTS 25: 25 meals per semester at '63 South and Knightro's.
   - KNIGHTS 50: 50 meals per semester; choose 400, 500, or 600 Dining Dollars per semester.
   - KNIGHTS 80: 80 meals per semester; choose 400, 500, or 600 Dining Dollars per semester.
- Stand-alone Dining Dollars plans are also offered (e.g., 775 Dining Dollars).

CHECKING BALANCES
Check Meal Swipes and Dining Dollars on a purchase receipt, at the meal plan office in Ferrell Commons (Mon–Fri 9 a.m.–5 p.m.), or by linking your UCF ID to your CampusDish/dining hub account online.
