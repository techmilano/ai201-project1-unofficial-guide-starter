Housing Options

UCF offers traditional suite-style and apartment-style housing on campus. All UCF Housing communities come with 24-hour staff support, furnished rooms, all utilities included, high-speed Wi-Fi, individual agreements, and central locations.

AGREEMENT TYPES AND COMMUNITIES

Academic-Year Agreement
A 2-semester agreement (Fall and Spring only), available for first-year students. Residents depart for winter break between semesters. Communities include a community center, laundry rooms, common areas, and access to a community kitchen:
- Apollo
- Nike and Hercules
- Lake Claire
- Libra
- Neptune

Annual Agreements
A 3-semester agreement (Fall, Spring, and the following Summer), available for first-year students and upperclassmen. Residents may remain in their rooms over semester breaks. These communities are apartment-style with private bedrooms, kitchens, and furnished living areas:
- Towers at Knights Plaza
- NorthView

Rosen Agreement
Includes Fall, Spring, and the option to add the following Summer — the only hybrid agreement. Available for first-year students and upperclassmen. Apartment-style rooms with full kitchens, living rooms, and a private community pool. The Rosen Campus is in Orlando's Entertainment District, about 30 minutes from main campus:
- Rosen College Apartments

AFFILIATED OFF-CAMPUS HOUSING
Affiliated properties are independently managed but affiliated with UCF. There are three:
- Knights Circle (near Main Campus): https://www.liveatknightscircle.com/
- The Pointe at Central (near Main Campus): https://www.thepointeatcentral.com/
- UnionWest Student Living (UCF Downtown Orlando Campus): https://unionweststudentliving.com/

CONFERENCE / INTERN HOUSING
Summer conference housing is available for students with Orlando-area internships and for organizations hosting overnight camps/conferences. See Conference Housing and Non-UCF Scholars/Interns pages.
