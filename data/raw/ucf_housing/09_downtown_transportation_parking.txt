Transportation Options & Parking — UCF Downtown

UCF Downtown is a transit-oriented campus offering many ways to get to and from campus: driving and carpools, express campus shuttles, public transit (SunRail, Lynx, Lymmo), and biking.

EXPRESS SHUTTLES
UCF Parking and Transportation Services runs 15 daily roundtrip express shuttles between the UCF main campus' Lynx Transit Center (between Garage A and Garage I) and UCF Downtown, open to UCF students and employees with a valid UCF ID. Hours: Monday–Thursday 6:30 a.m.–10:30 p.m. and Friday 6:30 a.m.–8:30 p.m., except holidays/closures. All shuttles have WiFi and are ADA compliant.

GROCERY SHUTTLE
Available to UCF and Valencia students from the downtown campus to the Publix at The Paramount on Lake Eola (400 E. Central Blvd.). Typically runs twice per month on Tuesdays with two roundtrips (departing campus 12:30 p.m. and 8:30 p.m.; departing Publix 1:45 p.m. and 9:45 p.m.). Pick-up/drop-off along Livingston Street in front of Dr. Phillips Academic Commons (528 W. Livingston Street). A valid UCF or Valencia Downtown ID is required.

PARKING
There are about 2,000 parking spaces supporting the downtown campus, including dedicated permit parking for students/faculty/staff, on-street and metered visitor spaces, and designated parking for UnionWest residents/visitors. Parking permits are required to park in UCF lots and garages, designated by permit type. There is NO overnight parking on the downtown campus or in the City garages at Creative Village.

- Students (Commuter): Commuting students purchase a Student "D" permit, allowing parking in all General (green) lots and unreserved garages. After 5:30 p.m., the permit also allows parking in Employee spaces unless noted.
- UnionWest Residential Students: The UnionWest garage is the only overnight parking option on-campus; each resident can lease one assigned space in the attached garage.
- Faculty/Staff: Employee permits allow parking in all designated Employee or General lots and unreserved garages.
- Guest/Visitor: Visitors may purchase an $8 daily virtual parking permit. The first level of the Parramore Garage is daily virtual permit parking only; overflow on the 4th floor of Parramore or any level of the Amelia Garage. Daily permits are valid in "D" (green) spaces in Parramore or Amelia; after 5:30 p.m., valid in red or blue spaces. Metered on-street parking on Livingston Street (and an hourly lot near the Marriott) is managed by the City of Orlando via the ParkMobile app. UnionWest also has hourly visitor parking.

KEY FAQ POINTS
- Safety escorts: Available to students, faculty, staff, and visitors by calling (407) 823-5555.
- Permit needed downtown? Yes; a UCF parking permit works at both the Parramore Avenue and Amelia Street garages and is valid at UCF Downtown AND the main campus.
- Extra cost downtown? No — your UCF parking permit is valid at UCF Downtown.
- No car? Get to main campus via the direct UCF shuttle, driving, the LYNX bus, or SunRail.
- Campus safety: UCF Downtown has a dedicated 24/7 police force in a sub-station on Amelia Street, with uniformed patrols, video surveillance, and blue-light phones.
